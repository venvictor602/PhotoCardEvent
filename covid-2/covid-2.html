Prevention Tips

Wear a mask

Wear a mask when physical distancing is not possible. Masks can help prevent the spread of the virus from the person.

Clean your hands

Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub.

Keep a safe distance

Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing. To practice social or physical distancing, stay at least 6 feet
